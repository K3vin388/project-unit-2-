Biodiversity

Summary

What is Biodiversity? Biodiversity is the variety of all living things from the smallest lving species to the largerst. Biodiversity changes overtime and is affected by many things like climate change, pollution, and habitat loss.

Why is this a problem?

Climate change, pollution, and habitat loss are all problems that affect biodiversity. Climate change causes many species to go extinct. Pollution causes many species to get sick and die. Habitat loss causes many species to lose their homes.

Why is this important?

Biodiversity is important for many reasons. It maintains clean water, clean air, and food. It also helps maintain the climate and the atmosphere. Biodiversity also helps maintain the water cycle and the nutrient cycle. Biodiversity is also imporant for human health, as it provides us with medicines.

How to solve this problem?

  • Reduce your carbon emissions
  • No littering
  • Plant Trees
  • Volunteer clean up
  • Reduce plastic consumption
  • Minimize food waiste
